TAO WANG

Welcome to my website!

I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Economics, University of Victoria (UVic). I like conceiving and developing novel research questions that are generally based on ideas in econometrics, statistics, and machine learning. My current research concentrates on building and developing a broad variety of parametric and nonparametric modal regression models and investigating their statistical inference with possible applications.

Research Interests

  • Econometric Theory
  • Nonparametric Statistics
  • Machine Learning
  • Applied Econometrics
